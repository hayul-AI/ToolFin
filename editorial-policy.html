Editorial Policy

Principles of clarity and neutrality.

Purpose

The primary purpose of ToolFin content is educational clarity. We aim to explain financial concepts through interactive modeling, allowing users to visualize mathematical relationships.

Neutral Tone

ToolFin maintains a strictly neutral tone. We do not provide financial advice or critiques of financial decisions. Our content avoids directive language and focuses on benchmarking and scenario explanation.

Sources

Our calculators and articles are based on standard financial mathematics and widely accepted economic principles. We rely on established formulas for amortization, compound interest, and investment returns.

Corrections Policy

If a calculation error or factual inaccuracy is identified, we prioritize its immediate correction. Users can report potential issues through our contact form. Every report is investigated to ensure the ongoing integrity of our tools.

Update Policy

We periodically review our tools to ensure they remain mathematically sound. Updates are documented, and our methodology page is adjusted to reflect any significant changes in how we approach financial modeling.
