How to Shop Smarter

Grocery stores often show the unit price on the shelf tag, but not always for every item or across different brands. Using a unit price calculator allows you to compare "bulk" deals against smaller packages to see if you're actually saving money.

Tip: Use Consistent Units

To get an accurate comparison, make sure you're using the same unit of measurement for both items (e.g., both in ounces, both in grams, or both by count).
