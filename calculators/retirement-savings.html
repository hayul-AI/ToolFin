Planning for Retirement

Retirement savings are built on the foundation of compound interest and time. By contributing regularly to your retirement accounts, you allow your money to grow exponentially. This calculator helps you see the long-term impact of starting early and maintaining consistent contributions.

The "4% Rule" shown in the results is a common benchmark used by retirees to determine a safe amount to withdraw from their portfolio each year without running out of money over a 30-year retirement period.
