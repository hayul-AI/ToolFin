What is Net Worth?

Net worth is arguably the single most important metric for tracking your long-term financial health. It is the total value of everything you own (your assets) minus the total of all your debts (your liabilities).

Tracking your net worth over time—rather than just your income—provides a true picture of your wealth building progress. Your goal is to grow your net worth by increasing your assets and decreasing your liabilities.
