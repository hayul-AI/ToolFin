What is an Overtime Calculator?

An overtime calculator helps employees understand their total earnings when working more than the standard hours. Most jurisdictions require a higher pay rate for overtime work, typically 1.5 times the normal hourly rate (Time-and-a-Half) or 2 times the normal rate (Double-Time).

This upcoming tool will allow you to enter your base pay and overtime hours to see the gross impact on your next paycheck.
