What is Self-Employment Tax?

Self-employment (SE) tax is a social security and Medicare tax primarily for individuals who work for themselves. It is similar to the FICA taxes withheld from the pay of most wage earners.

When you are an employee, your employer pays half of the FICA tax (7.65%) and you pay the other half. When you are self-employed, you are responsible for both the employer and employee portions, totaling 15.3%.
