Tipping Etiquette

Tipping is a common practice in many parts of the world, especially in North America, to show appreciation for good service. While standards vary, here are some general guidelines:

  • Standard Service: 15-18%
  • Excellent Service: 20% or more
  • Large Groups: Many restaurants automatically add an 18-20% gratuity for parties of 6 or more.
