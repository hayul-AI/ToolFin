What are Closing Costs?

Closing costs are the fees and expenses you pay when you close on your house, beyond the down payment. For buyers, they typically range from 2% to 5% of the home's purchase price.

Common Fee Categories

  • Lender Fees: Includes origination charges, discount points, and credit report fees.
  • Third-Party Fees: Appraisal, home inspection, title search, and title insurance.
  • Prepaid Items: Lenders often require you to pay for your first year of homeowners insurance and upfront property taxes at closing.
  • Government Fees: Recording fees and transfer taxes charged by your local or state government to document the sale.
