Why calculate take-home pay?

Understanding your net income—the amount that actually hits your bank account—is critical for effective budgeting. Your gross salary is often significantly different from your spending power due to various mandatory and voluntary deductions.

This upcoming tool will help you model different scenarios, such as the impact of increasing retirement contributions or moving to a different state with different tax rates.
